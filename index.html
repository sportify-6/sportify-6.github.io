ABOUT

Sports has gained tremendous popularity all over the globe and this healthy trend is likely to continue in the future also. Naturally political influence, gender discrimination and wealth inequalities between the rich and the poor in sports has gained over the years.Sportify reduces all these inequalities by supporting talented youths of all kinds of background and gender who has craving to achieve in sports.

Trainer is the sportsman, whom due to certain situations or politics cannot continue their career as a sportsman and this app helps them to continue their passion by helping them become a coach.

Trainee is the person who has limited access to information, resources, opportunities and programmes for participation, training and competition.

The ultimate aim of this web application is to provide the talented youth with reasonable support given by the coach to train the trainee through interaction.
